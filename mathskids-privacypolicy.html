Privacy Policy for MathsKids

Last Updated: June 2026

MathsKids ("the App") is an educational game designed for children and families. We are committed to protecting the privacy and safety of our users and complying with applicable child privacy requirements and Google Play Families Policy requirements.

1. Information We Collect

MathsKids does not require users to create an account.

We do not knowingly collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Home Address
  • Photos
  • Videos
  • Contacts
  • Precise Location Information

2. Children's Privacy

MathsKids is intended for children and families.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

3. Advertising

MathsKids may display advertisements through Google AdMob.

The App is configured to support child-directed experiences and, where required, requests child-appropriate and non-personalized advertising.

Advertising providers may collect limited technical information necessary to:

  • Serve advertisements
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Maintain service functionality
  • Measure advertising performance

For more information regarding Google's privacy practices, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. In-App Purchases

MathsKids may offer optional in-app purchases.

All purchases are processed securely through Google Play. MathsKids does not collect, receive, or store payment card information.

5. Local Device Storage

The App stores certain information locally on the user's device in order to provide game functionality, including:

  • Game Progress
  • Earned Stars
  • Achievements
  • Selected Cat Character
  • Audio Preferences
  • Gameplay Preferences
  • Unlocked Content
  • App Settings

This information remains on the user's device and is not transmitted to us.

6. Third-Party Services

MathsKids may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing

These services operate under their own privacy policies and may process limited technical information necessary for their operation.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information used by the App and maintain a safe experience for users.

8. Data Deletion

Users may delete locally stored game data by:

  • Using the in-app Reset Progress feature
  • Uninstalling the App

9. Parents and Guardians

Parents and guardians may contact us regarding questions about privacy practices or requests relating to information stored by the App.

10. Compliance

MathsKids is designed to comply with applicable Google Play Families Policy requirements and child privacy obligations.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page together with an updated revision date.
