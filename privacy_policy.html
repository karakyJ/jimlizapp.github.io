Privacy Policy

Kids Truth or Dare

Effective Date: May 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using Kids Truth or Dare (“the App”).

The App is designed for local multiplayer gameplay for children ages 5–10 and is intended to be used under parental supervision.

1. Who We Are

App Name: Kids Truth or Dare

Developer: JimLizApp

Package Name: com.karakyj.truthordare

Contact Email: karaky@gmail.com

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit personal information.

  • Names, email addresses, or personal identifiers
  • Location data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone recordings
  • Contacts or calendars
  • Browsing history or search history
  • Social media information
  • Personal information from children

The App does not require account creation, login, or registration.

All gameplay information is stored locally on the device only and is not transmitted to external servers.

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores certain gameplay information locally on the device using Android SharedPreferences.

  • Player names and selected emojis
  • App settings and sound preferences
  • Unlocked packs and wheel themes
  • Previously shown prompts
  • Purchase status for unlocked content

This information remains on the device only and can be removed by:

  • Using the “Reset Everything” option in the Parent Zone
  • Uninstalling the App

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

4a. Google AdMob

The free version of the App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob (Google LLC).

Ad requests are configured as child-directed in accordance with Google Play Families requirements.

  • Personalised advertising is disabled
  • Behavioural targeting is disabled
  • Interest-based advertising is disabled
  • Remarketing is disabled

More information about Google advertising technologies is available at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4b. Other Third-Party Services

The App does not use analytics SDKs, social media SDKs, or external tracking services.

4c. Removing Ads

Advertisements can be removed through optional in-app purchases.

5. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases such as additional packs, premium access, and cosmetic customisation items.

All purchases are processed securely through Google Play Billing. The developer does not collect or store payment information.

6. COPPA Compliance (Children Under 13)

This App is directed to children under the age of 13.

In accordance with COPPA:

  • The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • The App does not require personal information for gameplay
  • Advertising is configured as child-directed
  • Behavioural advertising is disabled
  • Parents can reset locally stored gameplay information through the Parent Zone

If you believe personal information has been collected unintentionally, please contact: karaky@gmail.com

7. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the App is designed to minimise data collection and use non-personalised advertising only.

Questions regarding GDPR-related rights may be sent to: karaky@gmail.com

8. Parent Zone

The App includes a Parent Zone protected by a mathematics question gate.

Parents can:

  • Enable or disable sound and voice features
  • Reset prompts and gameplay data
  • Reset app settings
  • Manage unlocked content

9. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit personal information, all gameplay information remains stored locally on the device.

10. Data Retention

Locally stored gameplay information remains on the device until:

  • The user resets the App data
  • The App is uninstalled

No gameplay information is stored on external servers.

11. App Features

  • Offline local multiplayer gameplay
  • No chat or social communication features
  • No user-generated public content
  • No external website links within gameplay
  • No account registration required
  • Parent Zone access protection

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the App or applicable laws.

The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are made.
