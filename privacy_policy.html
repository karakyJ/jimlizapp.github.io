Truth or Dare: Parent Approved

Thank you for choosing Truth or Dare: Parent Approved ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you or your child uses our App. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and complying with all applicable privacy laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

This App is designed for children ages 5–10 and is intended to be used under parental supervision.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We are committed to a privacy-first approach. This App does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Location data (GPS or otherwise)
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Device contacts or calendars
  • Browsing history or search history
  • Social media information
  • Any personal information from children

The App does not require account creation, login, or registration of any kind.

All game data (player names entered, settings, and game progress) is stored locally on the device only and never transmitted to any server.

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on the device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves the device:

  • Player names and emoji entered by users (for gameplay only)
  • App settings such as sound preferences and unlocked packs
  • A record of which prompts have been shown (to avoid repetition)
  • Purchase status for unlocked content

This data can be cleared at any time by using the "Reset Everything" option in the Parent Zone, or by uninstalling the App.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

4a. Google AdMob

The free version of the App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob (Google LLC). We have configured all ad requests as child-directed in compliance with COPPA and Google's Families Policy. This means:

  • Ads are served without personalisation or behavioural targeting
  • Interest-based advertising is disabled
  • Remarketing is disabled
  • No persistent identifiers are used for advertising purposes

Google AdMob may collect certain non-personal technical information to serve contextual ads. To learn more about how Google handles data in child-directed contexts, please visit: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4b. No Other Third-Party SDKs

The App does not use analytics SDKs, social media SDKs, crash reporting tools, or any other third-party data collection services beyond AdMob for advertising.

4c. Removing Ads

Users can remove all advertisements by purchasing the Premium or Ultra Premium in-app purchase. Once purchased, no ads will be shown and AdMob will not serve any ad requests.

5. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases (packs, premium tiers, and wheel skins). These purchases are processed entirely through Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information. All billing is handled by Google Play's billing system, subject to Google's own Privacy Policy.

⚠️ COPPA COMPLIANCE

6. COPPA Compliance (Children Under 13)

This App is directed to children under the age of 13. We take COPPA compliance seriously. In accordance with COPPA:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do not require children to provide personal information to use the App
  • All advertising is configured as child-directed, disabling personalised ads
  • No behavioural advertising or tracking is used
  • Parents can review, modify, or delete any locally stored data via the Parent Zone in the App

If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, please contact us immediately at karaky@gmail.com and we will take immediate steps to delete such information.

7. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we do not collect personal data from users, the processing activities covered by this policy are minimal. The App is designed to be privacy-by-default and privacy-by-design.

For advertising, we use only contextual, non-personalised advertising as required for apps directed at children under 16 under GDPR.

If you have questions about your rights under GDPR, please contact us at karaky@gmail.com.

8. Parental Controls

The App includes a Parent Zone protected by a mathematics question gate. From the Parent Zone, parents can:

  • Turn sound and voice features on or off
  • Lock sound settings so children cannot change them
  • Reset used prompts to refresh the question pool
  • Reset all app data and settings
  • Manage premium purchases and unlocked content

We strongly encourage parents to review the App with their children and to use the Parent Zone to configure the experience appropriately.

9. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit personal data, the risk of a data breach affecting user privacy is minimal. All locally stored data is protected by the standard security mechanisms of the Android operating system.

10. Data Retention

All data stored by the App exists only on the user's device. It is retained until the user chooses to reset it via the Parent Zone, or until the App is uninstalled. We do not retain any data on external servers.

11. Children's Safety

This App was designed with children's safety as the highest priority:

  • No internet connection required to play
  • No social features, chat, or user-generated content
  • No links to external websites within the game
  • No registration or account creation required
  • All questions and dares were carefully reviewed by parents
  • No romantic, violent, or inappropriate content
  • Parent Zone protects access to settings

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable laws. When we make significant changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this policy. We encourage parents to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
