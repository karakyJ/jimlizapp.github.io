Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 8, 2026

Truth or Dare Kids is a local multiplayer party game designed for children ages 5–10. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the app.

1. Developer Information

Developer Name: JimLizApp
Contact Email: karaky@gmail.com

2. Information Collection

Truth or Dare Kids does not require account creation or user registration. The app does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, location data, contacts, photos, or microphone recordings.

Gameplay data remains stored locally on the device only.

3. Local Device Storage

The app may locally store:

  • Player nicknames
  • Sound settings
  • Unlocked packs and themes
  • Previously shown prompts
  • Purchase status

This information stays on the device and can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

4. Advertising

The app may display ads provided by Google AdMob. Ads are configured for child-directed treatment in accordance with Google Play Families policies. Personalised advertising is disabled.

More information: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. In-App Purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases such as additional packs, themes, and premium content. Purchases are securely processed through Google Play Billing.

6. Children’s Privacy

Truth or Dare Kids is intended for children and family gameplay. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe information has been collected unintentionally, contact: karaky@gmail.com

7. Parent Zone

The app includes a Parent Zone where parents can manage settings, reset local gameplay data, and control sound features.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any updates will be posted on this page.
